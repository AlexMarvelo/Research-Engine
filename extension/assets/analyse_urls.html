Importing History & Bookmarks

Step 1: Analysing size & download time

By default, the plugin only stores pages that you visit after installing the plugin.

To make all your previously visited pages & your bookmarks available, we need to download them first.
This might take a while and influence your browsing speed.

You can always pause and continue the download process.
