About the WorldBrain Project

Our Vision

WorldBrain is an open-source project that aims to battle (scientific) misinformation by developing a quality score for web content/sources and an expert ranking for users/authors/scientists in a given field.

Our first step is a personalisable search-engine that lets users full-text searches on the pages they visited and bookmarked.
In later stages users can voluntarily share metadata with their friends, the community and WorldBrain.
We analyse this data in order to develop the quality scores and uncover related content. (i.e. counter perspectives or unsourced studies)

The tool is built on the basis of Falcon, another open-source tool that provides the initial history search functionality.

Read more about it in our project overview.

What happens with my data?

We have no access to your data and won't ever have without your prior approval. Your data truly stays yours.
In later stages you can voluntarily share parts of your metadata with your friends, the community and WorldBrain.
